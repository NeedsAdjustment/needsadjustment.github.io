Little Bit of Luke

I knew a boy
Fresh off the boat,
Whomst did enjoy
My poems wrote

His name was Luke,
And Luke he was
But just a little
Herein because

As is his wont
In sad music he sits
Lets emo Luke out
But just in little bits

Cos when he does
He must let flow
Only a little emo luke
Lest too depressed he grow
